Welcome to Rate My Modules...

By Students, For Students.

We let you both rate your previous modules and read the thoughts and opinions of others to help you decide upon which modules you would like to take.

To view the reviews of modules simply click on the "Search" button above. To write your own review please register an account and login. You have to register an account using your University provided email address to verify you really are a student at that university.

House Rules

All reviews are anonymous but we ask you follow our house rules, your account may be suspended if you abuse the system.

  • Reviews should be accurate and honest.
  • No swearing or discrimination of any kind. Keep it civil.
  • No overly targeted or rude reviews about specific module conveners.
  • Don't create fake modules.

If you find any content you believe is offensive please report it for review.


Supported Universities:

We currently only support a small number of Universities but hope to support more as we grow.
